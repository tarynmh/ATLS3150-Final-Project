Welcome to Backpack Society

where happy hearts and bright futures start with full bellies.

​

At Backpack Society we’re raising funds and collecting food donations to serve our neighbors who need it most.

​

We partner with local schools to reach students in need. Please join us by supporting our efforts to make a measurable difference in the lives of students and their families in our community.

​​

Backpack Society partners with schools in Douglas County School District, Littleton Public Schools, and Cherry Creek School District.

About

A bit of background...
​
Here at Backpack Society, we are driven by a single goal; to do our part in removing food insecurity as an obstacle for children so they can grow, learn, and thrive.
​ ​

WHO WE ARE

We are advocates, partners, and volunteers dedicated to changing the lives of students, their families, and school staff. ​
100% Volunteer Organization

WHAT WE DO

We offer hope through a consistent bag of easy-to-prepare meals on the weekend so kids can focus on important things - like being a kid. We also offer weekly groceries to their entire family.

WHY WE DO IT

The most at-risk students for food insecurity are also the ones who are most affected by the achievement gap, and poor academic outcomes that can have lifelong effects. By providing weekend food, we hope students will be well fed over the weekend and can come in Monday mornings, ready to learn.
